Unmatched Career analysis

Bolt made his professional running career Debut in 2004 after setting a new World Junior Record in the 200 at 19.93 seconds. He went on to qualify for the 2004 Olympics but got 40th in the 200. His professional career in the following years was very impressive, winning several silver medals in world level races. Bolt was just getting started, however, becasue in the 2008 Olympics, his second Olympics ever, Bolt set new world and Olympic records in both the 100 and 200 meter dashes. Bolt would later improve on both of these times in the 2009 world championships setting new and still standing world records for both events. Bolt would also set the 4x100 m relay world record with his team in the 2012 Olympic Games. Bolt is considered to be the best track and field athelte of all time, not just becasue pf his impressive world and Olympic records; from his first Olympic gold medal in 2008 till his retirement in 2017, Bolt took first in every world championship or Olympic race he participated in. There may never be another athlete as successful as Usain bolt.
